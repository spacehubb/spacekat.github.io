Artist Bio

Spacekat is a dynamic singer and performer whose music blends the rich essence of reggae with soulful Afro alternative fusion. With a voice that resonates deep emotion and a style that is both ethereal and grounded.

Whether she's offering catharsis for heartbreak, affirmation for the weary, or sonic space for spiritual awakening, Spacekat weaves messages of divine connection into every song.
